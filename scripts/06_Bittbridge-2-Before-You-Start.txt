The key concepts here. What is a virtual machine? Think of it as a remote laptop or remote PC, hosted specifically on Google Cloud, that runs 24/7. It doesn't care whether you switch it on or off, or whether you're using it — you can set up scripts and it will keep working without your interaction.

What is a virtual environment? It's a self-contained environment for your project or your Python scripts, with its own dependencies, isolated from the global environment on your laptop. Think of it as a specific folder inside your project where we keep all the necessary libraries and variables, just for this environment.
